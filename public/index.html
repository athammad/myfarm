Davide Vaccari

Davide Vaccari is an economics student at University of Ferrara with a background in IT. He owns and manages a farm. He’s the winner of the “Youth in Action” national competition in Italy and the founder of Smart Farming.

Soheil Shayegh

Soheil Shayegh has a PhD in Industrial and Systems Engineering with a background in Civil Engineering . He has more than 10 years of international experience working on research and development projects in various countries in the mIddle East, Africa, North America, and Europe. He is currently based in Milano, Italy working as a researcher at Bocconi University. His research interests include the impacts of climate change on agricultural sector and labor supply.

Davide Mazzoni

Davide Mazzoni is Junior Researcher at Fondazione Eni Enrico Mattei. His research activity is focused on digitalization, smart agriculture and energy access in Sub-Saharan Africa. He holds a MSc in Energy Engineering from the Polytechnic University of Turin, where he studied innovation in renewable energy sources and energy efficiency. Keen on agriculture, he implemented technological solutions into the family farm in order to reduce the GHG emissions and shape a more sustainable business model. After his graduation he also had an experience in the electronic industry as Embedded System Architect.

Francesco Amato

Francesco Amato has a Ph.D in Electrical and Computer Engineering and several years of academic and professional experience on wireless communications and microwave engineering. He pioneered work in long-range and low-powered RFID backscattering communications and is currently a researcher at the Sant’Anna School of Advanced Studies in Pisa. His research interests include wireless sensing and microwave photonics.

Laura Cavalli

Laura Cavalli has a PhD in Economics and Finance of Public Administration. After more than ten years spent in academic research working on social issues employing both quantitative and qualitative methods, from September 2017 she is senior researcher at Fondazione Eni Enrico Mattei, working on Sustainable Development and local innovative solutions to fulfil the Agenda 2030; moreover, she is United Nations Sustainable Development Solutions Network Manager for Italy.
